# Job Title: Director of Engineering - Core Fulfillment

We are looking for a skilled Director of Engineering Core Fulfillment to join
our remote team. You will help us in ensuring our engineering team delivers
software designed, built, and maintained to the highest standards to enable our
growth.

## First 90 Days Output/Goals
- Meet with all of the existing staff and conduct hand off of personnel management from their existing manager.
- Recruit and hire engineering managers for their engineering pod
- Work with each engineering team to define roadmap and get their first project fully scoped, estimated, and approved by the Executive Leadership Team.
- Scope and fully estimate the next two development efforts for each team.

## Job Description

- Location: Remote 
- Department: Engineering 
- Report to: VP of Engineering 

We are seeking a strategic and hands-on Director of Engineering to lead our growing engineering organization. This role is responsible for scaling teams, ensuring agile processes are followed, ensuring development and infrastructure standards are followed, and measuring outcomes to iterate on processes. You will work closely with product, design, and business leaders to define the engineering roadmap and foster a culture of innovation, accountability, and collaboration. You will be primarily concerned with ensuring smooth execution of complex deliverables across multiple engineering teams. Our core fulfillment team builds the platform that powers our main direct to consumer brand and provides services to our other business units.

Leadership & Strategy
- Define the long-term vision and technical strategy in alignment with business goals.
- Build, mentor, and manage high-performing multiple engineering teams.
- Drive cross-functional collaboration with product, design, and operations teams.

Execution & Delivery
- Oversee planning, execution, and delivery of major product initiatives.
- Establish and monitor KPIs to track team performance and product quality.
- Ensure scalable, secure, and reliable systems are built and maintained.

People & Culture
- Foster an open culture of ownership, transparency, and continuous improvement.
- Lead hiring and talent development, ensuring career growth and retention.

Technology & Innovation
- Stay current with industry trends and emerging technologies to inform strategy.
- Encourage innovation and experimentation while balancing delivery commitments.
- Guide architectural decisions and ensure adherence to best practices.

## How we work

- Ideas and priorities flow from across the company.
- Big initiatives are planned into 4-8 week releases.
- Progress is tracked in two-week cycles.
- Merged PRs auto-deploy to staging and production via chatops.
- Recorded work in progress demos are shared with the company.

## Key responsibilities

- Write code for technical deliverables, without blocking or slowing down the progress of your team
- Empower the engineering team to develop scalable, reliable, and maintainable systems
- Contribute via leveraged output activities -- one-on-ones, training, pair programming, and adding
  CI checks.
- Promote good engineering practices within the team and beyond
- Identify and implement improvements to our products and processes to support our growth
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure technical solutions align with product strategy
  and reasonable timelines
- Ensure team members understand how the work they are doing ties to wider company goals
- Manage a remote team across multiple timezones
- Nurture a healthy team culture by
  - being a role model for the behaviors you wish to see,
  - coach team members around work and personal growth,
  - seek to create and support a diverse and inclusive team, and
  - monitor team health and morale and ensure improvements
- Foster a continuous learning and improvement mindset within your team. Ensure team members have
  the skills needed for their role
- Be a sponsor for engineers so they have access to growth opportunities they wouldn't otherwise
  have on their own
