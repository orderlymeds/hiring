# Job Title: Director of Security Operations

We are looking for a skilled Director of Security Operations to join our remote team. You will help
us in ensuring our engineering team delivers software designed, built, and maintained to the
highest standards to enable our growth.

## First 90 Days Output/Goals

- Baseline & Risk Assessment: Conduct a comprehensive review of current security operations (SOC,
  tooling, processes, incident history, and physical security controls at both sites) and deliver a
  prioritized risk report to the CISO.
- Operational Playbooks & Incident Readiness: Develop or refine incident response playbooks,
  escalation paths, and on-call procedures; run at least one tabletop exercise with key
  stakeholders to validate readiness.
- Visibility & Metrics: Implement or enhance monitoring dashboards and KPIs (coverage, detection
  rates, MTTR, patch/vulnerability status) to establish measurable security posture tracking for
  ongoing reporting.
