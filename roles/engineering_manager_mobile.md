# Job Title: Engineering Manager - Mobile

We are looking for a skilled Engineering Manager, Mobile, to join our
remote team. You will help us in ensuring our engineering team delivers
software designed, built, and maintained to the highest standards to enable our
growth.

## First 90 Days Output/Goals

- Meet with all of the existing staff and conduct hand off of personnel
  management from their existing manager.
- Establish a regular cadence of one-on-ones with their staff and their peer
  leaders
- Lead weekly agile processes for their team – standups, backlog grooming,
  sprint planning, retrospectives, and demonstrations.
- Meet with vendors and contractors that work alongside their teams and ensure
  good vendor management for those partners.
- Prepare and present weekly team updates to engineering and product leadership
  and be present for Q&A with leadership. This should include risk analysis for
  the ongoing and upcoming project work.
- Work ahead their team to ensure a pipeline 2-3 fully planned roadmap releases
  ahead of the current release in development.
- Review and establish the testing strategy and test plans for the ongoing work
  of their team. Onboard a QA analyst to work with their team and ensure the
  process with QA to iterate on test plans.

## Job Description

- Location: Remote 
- Department: Engineering 
- Report to: Director of Engineering 

As an Engineering Manager, you’ll focus on empowering and coaching a
distributed team of engineers to deliver high-quality, scalable, and
maintainable systems. You’ll create leverage through mentorship, one-on-ones,
pair programming, training, and thoughtful process improvements—helping the
team grow their skills, confidence, and impact. You’ll promote strong
engineering practices, align technical execution with product strategy and
timelines, and ensure each engineer understands how their work connects to the
company’s broader goals. You’ll nurture a healthy, inclusive, and
high-performing team culture by modeling the behaviors you want to see,
supporting personal and professional growth, and monitoring team health to
drive continuous improvement. Ultimately, you’ll foster an environment of
learning, accountability, and shared purpose where engineers can do their best
work.

Key responsibilities
- Empower the engineering team to develop scalable, reliable, and maintainable systems
- Contribute via leveraged output activities -- one-on-ones, training, pair programming, and adding CI checks.
- Promote good engineering and operations practices within the team and beyond
- Identify and implement improvements to our products and processes to support our growth
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure technical solutions align with product strategy and reasonable timelines
- Ensure team members understand how the work they are doing ties to wider company goals
- Manage a remote team across multiple time-zones
- Conduct and enforce agile processes for your team
- Nurture a healthy team culture by
  - Being a role model for the behaviors you wish to see 
  - Coaching team members around work and personal growth
  - Seeking to create and support a diverse and inclusive team
  - Monitoring team health and morale and ensure continuous improvement
- Foster a continuous learning and improvement mindset within your team. Ensure team members have the skills needed for their role
- Be a sponsor for engineers so they have access to growth opportunities they wouldn't otherwise have on their own
